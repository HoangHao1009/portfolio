TIKI WEBSITE CRAWLER

Business Understanding

In the current business context, an understanding about market and product is extreme important. This project help you to collect data from E-commerce flatform Tiki. So, you can analyze it to have a sense of your industry/ product segment

This project is built for easy-used. You just put a link of category in Tiki and receive results

How is it built?

+ Crawling: use Selenium running parallely by Threading

+ Wrangling data: use Pandas, re

How it can be used?

+ Crawl just one category you chose: use .crawl_multipage()

+ Crawl detail category in category you chose: use .super_crawling(). This will take so much information for you but also take more time/ resources
