MARKETING AUTO ANALYZER PROJECT

Business Understanding

As a marketing/data analyst. I often go through repeated analysis on several issues. Therefore, a tool that gives analysts a basic, comprehensive view of a field is necessary to help save time and brainpower. This project is that tool for marketing (customer) analysis.

Analytics Approach

This project focus on:

+ Marketing metrics: Revenue, Total customer, New-Existing Customer, Retention, ...

+ Customer Segmentation: segment by RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary Value).

+ Prediction: Customer Life Time Value.

Data preparation

For users, you can upload your marketing data that have columns: customer, sales, segment, date or you can use sample data to see how the tool perform.

Modelling

+ Unsupervised learning: KMeans Clustering for Customer Segmentation.

+ Supervised learning: Combine a variety of techniques from traditional (Decision Tree, SVM, ...) to modern (Random Forest, XGBoost, Multi Layer Perceptron,...) to chose what is the best predictor for your data.
